Open Screen offers extensive customization options to match your brand and create professional-looking screen recordings. This guide covers all visual customization features.

## Background Customization

The background surrounds your video content and sets the visual tone for your recording.

### Using Built-in Wallpapers

Open Screen includes 18 professionally designed wallpapers:

1. Open the **Settings Panel** (right sidebar)
2. Expand **Background** section
3. Select the **Image** tab
4. Click any wallpaper to apply it

<Tip>
Wallpapers are categorized by color tone and style. Preview each to find what complements your content best.
</Tip>

### Solid Colors

For minimalist or brand-matched backgrounds:

1. Open **Background** section
2. Select **Color** tab
3. Choose from 16 preset colors:
   - Red, Yellow, Green, White
   - Blue, Orange, Purple, Pink
   - Cyan, Deep Orange, Light Green, Amber
   - Brand Green, Black, Blue Grey, Brown
4. Click any color to apply

**Use Cases:**
- **Black (#000000)** - Professional, high contrast
- **White (#FFFFFF)** - Clean, documentation-friendly
- **Brand Green (#34B27B)** - Open Screen branded
- **Custom colors** - Match your brand palette

### Gradient Backgrounds

24 beautiful gradient presets for dynamic backgrounds:

1. Open **Background** section
2. Select **Gradient** tab
3. Preview and select from:
   - Warm gradients (orange, yellow, red tones)
   - Cool gradients (blue, cyan, purple tones)
   - Multi-color gradients
   - Subtle to dramatic transitions

<Note>
Gradients are rendered during export, ensuring smooth transitions and high quality output.
</Note>

### Custom Images

Upload your own background images:

1. Click **Upload Custom** in the Image tab
2. Select a JPG or JPEG file
3. Image is loaded instantly
4. Remove custom images by clicking the **X** icon

**Image Requirements:**
- Format: JPG or JPEG only
- Recommended size: 1920×1080 or higher
- Aspect ratio: Should match or exceed your video

<Warning>
Custom images are stored in the project file. Large images increase project file size.
</Warning>

## Video Effects

Effects enhance the visual presentation of your recorded content.

### Shadow

Add depth with drop shadow:

**Setting:** 0-100% intensity

**Common Settings:**
- **0%** - No shadow (flat design)
- **30-50%** - Subtle shadow (professional)
- **70-100%** - Dramatic shadow (emphasis)

**Best for:**
- Product demos with brand backgrounds
- Tutorial videos
- Social media content

### Background Blur

Blur the background wallpaper for focus effect:

**Toggle:** On/Off

**Effect:**
- Applies Gaussian blur to background
- Video remains sharp
- Creates depth-of-field effect

**Best for:**
- Busy or colorful backgrounds
- Emphasizing video content
- Professional presentations

### Motion Blur

Add realistic motion blur:

**Toggle:** On/Off

**Effect:**
- Simulates camera motion blur
- Smooths fast cursor movements
- Makes animations more natural

**Best for:**
- Fast-paced demos
- Cursor-heavy tutorials
- Smooth, cinematic feel

<Info>
Motion blur is calculated during export and doesn't affect preview performance.
</Info>

### Roundness

Round video corners:

**Setting:** 0-16px border radius

**Common Settings:**
- **0px** - Sharp corners (traditional)
- **8px** - Subtle rounding (modern)
- **12px** - Noticeable rounding (polished)
- **16px** - Maximum rounding (very rounded)

**Best for:**
- Modern, sleek appearance
- Social media content
- Brand-aligned design

### Padding

Control space between video and background:

**Setting:** 0-100% padding

**Visual Impact:**
- **0%** - Video fills entire frame
- **25%** - Small border
- **50%** - Balanced composition (recommended)
- **75%** - Large border
- **100%** - Maximum padding

**Best for:**
- Showcasing backgrounds
- Creating breathing room
- Professional presentation style

## Aspect Ratio

Choose your video dimensions from the timeline toolbar:

### Available Ratios

**16:9 (Landscape)**
- Standard widescreen
- YouTube, Vimeo, presentations
- Most versatile option

**9:16 (Portrait)**
- Vertical video
- Instagram Stories, TikTok, mobile
- Full-screen mobile viewing

**1:1 (Square)**
- Social media posts
- Instagram feed, LinkedIn
- Balanced composition

**4:3 (Classic)**
- Traditional format
- Documentation
- Legacy compatibility

**21:9 (Ultrawide)**
- Cinematic format
- Ultrawide displays
- Premium feel

**3:4 (Vertical Classic)**
- Portrait with classic proportions
- Mobile-friendly
- Tall displays

<Tip>
Change aspect ratio anytime - it's non-destructive. Export the same recording in multiple ratios for different platforms.
</Tip>

## Crop Control

Crop your video to focus on specific areas:

1. Click **Crop Video** in Video Effects section
2. Drag edges to adjust crop region:
   - **Top** - Crop from top
   - **Bottom** - Crop from bottom
   - **Left** - Crop from left
   - **Right** - Crop from right
3. Click **Done** to apply

**Crop Use Cases:**
- Remove taskbars or menus
- Focus on specific window area
- Exclude private/sensitive information
- Create tighter framing

<Note>
Crop is applied in normalized coordinates (0-1), so it scales correctly with any aspect ratio.
</Note>

## Annotation Styling

### Text Customization

When an annotation is selected, customize text appearance:

**Font Styles (Built-in):**
- **Classic** - System UI (clean, readable)
- **Editor** - Georgia serif (editorial)
- **Strong** - Impact (bold, attention-grabbing)
- **Typewriter** - Courier New (monospace)
- **Deco** - Brush Script (decorative)
- **Simple** - Arial (universal)
- **Modern** - Verdana (contemporary)
- **Clean** - Trebuchet MS (minimalist)

**Font Sizes:**
- 12px to 128px
- Common: 24px, 32px, 48px, 64px

**Text Formatting:**
- **Bold** - Increase weight
- **Italic** - Slant text
- **Underline** - Add underline

**Text Alignment:**
- Left, Center, Right

### Custom Fonts

Add your brand fonts:

1. Select a text annotation
2. Click **Add Custom Font**
3. Enter font details:
   - Font name (display name)
   - Font family (CSS font-family value)
   - Font URL (hosted .ttf, .otf, or .woff file)
4. Click **Add Font**
5. Font appears in dropdown

<Accordion title="Setting up custom fonts">
**Font URL Requirements:**
- Must be publicly accessible
- Supported formats: TTF, OTF, WOFF, WOFF2
- Use CDN or Google Fonts URL

**Example:**
```
Name: Roboto Bold
Family: 'Roboto', sans-serif
URL: https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Roboto:wght@700&display=swap
```

Custom fonts are loaded during export for consistent rendering.
</Accordion>

### Color Customization

**Text Color:**
- 16 preset colors
- Click color swatch to choose
- High contrast recommended for readability

**Background Color:**
- Transparent (default)
- 16 preset colors
- Click **Clear Background** to remove

**Popular Combinations:**
- White text + transparent background (classic)
- Black text + white background (high contrast)
- White text + brand color background (branded)
- Colored text + transparent background (subtle)

### Arrow Styling

Customize arrow annotations:

**Direction:** 8 options
- Up, Down, Left, Right
- Up-Right, Up-Left, Down-Right, Down-Left

**Stroke Width:** 1-6px
- 2-3px: Subtle
- 4-5px: Standard
- 6px: Bold

**Color:** 16 preset options
- Match brand colors
- High contrast for visibility

## Export Settings

Customize output quality and format:

### MP4 Export

**Quality Levels:**

**Low (720p)**
- Resolution: 1280×720 (landscape)
- Bitrate: 10 Mbps
- File size: Smallest
- Best for: Quick sharing, email, web

**Medium (1080p)**
- Resolution: 1920×1080 (landscape)
- Bitrate: 20 Mbps
- File size: Balanced
- Best for: Most use cases, YouTube, documentation

**High (Source)**
- Resolution: Source resolution preserved
- Bitrate: 30-80 Mbps (based on resolution)
- File size: Largest
- Best for: 4K displays, archival, professional work

<Note>
Actual export dimensions maintain your selected aspect ratio. Values shown are for 16:9.
</Note>

### GIF Export

**Frame Rate:**
- **10 fps** - Smallest file size
- **12 fps** - Balanced
- **15 fps** - Smooth (recommended)
- **20 fps** - Smoothest, larger files

**Size Presets:**
- **Small (Sm)** - Optimized for file size
- **Medium (Med)** - Balanced (1280px width)
- **Original (Orig)** - Full resolution

**Loop:** Enable for continuous playback

**GIF Best Practices:**
- Keep duration under 10 seconds
- Use 12-15 fps for best file size
- Medium size for documentation
- Enable loop for repeating content

## Creating Brand Presets

Save time by creating reusable brand styles:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure your brand style">
    Set up:
    - Brand background color or wallpaper
    - Consistent padding (e.g., 50%)
    - Shadow intensity (e.g., 40%)
    - Border radius (e.g., 8px)
    - Aspect ratio (e.g., 16:9)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add brand elements">
    - Upload logo as image annotation
    - Create text annotation with brand fonts
    - Set brand colors
    - Position elements
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save as template project">
    Click **Save Project** and name it:
    - "Brand Template - 16:9"
    - "Brand Template - 1:1"
    - "Brand Template - Story"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Reuse for new recordings">
    1. **Load Project** your template
    2. Record or load new video
    3. Template style is applied
    4. Adjust timeline edits
    5. Export with consistent branding
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Style Recommendations by Use Case

### Product Demos
```
Background: Gradient or brand color
Shadow: 40-50%
Blur: Enabled
Roundness: 8-12px
Padding: 50%
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
```

### Documentation / Help Articles
```
Background: White or light gray
Shadow: 30%
Blur: Disabled
Roundness: 4px
Padding: 40%
Aspect Ratio: 16:9 or 4:3
Format: GIF (short clips) or MP4
```

### Social Media
```
Background: Brand gradient or color
Shadow: 60%
Blur: Enabled
Roundness: 12px
Padding: 60%
Aspect Ratio: 9:16 (Stories) or 1:1 (Posts)
Quality: Medium
```

### Tutorial Videos
```
Background: Solid dark color
Shadow: 40%
Blur: Disabled
Roundness: 8px
Padding: 45%
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Quality: Medium or High
```

### Professional Presentations
```
Background: Professional wallpaper or solid
Shadow: 50%
Blur: Enabled
Roundness: 8px
Padding: 50%
Aspect Ratio: 16:9 or 21:9
Quality: High
```

<Warning>
Always preview your style before recording or exporting. Visual preferences vary by platform and audience.
</Warning>
