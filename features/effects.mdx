## Overview

Open Screen provides a suite of visual effects to give your recordings a polished, professional appearance. All effects are applied in real-time during export with hardware acceleration support.

## Background Wallpapers

Add aesthetic backgrounds to frame your recording instead of a plain color.

### Using Wallpapers

1. Open the **Effects** panel on the right side
2. Browse through available wallpaper options
3. Click a wallpaper to apply it instantly
4. The video preview updates in real-time

<Info>
Wallpapers are rendered behind your video content with automatic scaling and positioning to match your export resolution.
</Info>

### Wallpaper Library

Open Screen includes built-in gradient wallpapers:

- Gradient backgrounds in various color schemes
- Optimized for different aspect ratios
- Located in the `assets/wallpapers/` directory
- Custom wallpapers can be added to the assets folder

## Shadow Effects

Add depth to your video with customizable drop shadows.

### Shadow Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Shadow">
    Adjust the shadow intensity slider from 0 (no shadow) to 100 (maximum shadow).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Preview">
    The video preview shows the shadow effect in real-time.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Export">
    Shadows are rendered during export, adding a subtle depth effect around your video.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Shadow Intensity">
    - **0** - No shadow (disabled)
    - **1-30** - Subtle shadow for light backgrounds
    - **31-70** - Medium shadow (recommended)
    - **71-100** - Strong shadow for dramatic effect
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Shadow Rendering">
    Shadows are rendered as WebGL effects with:
    - Soft gaussian blur
    - Automatic color adjustment based on background
    - Optimized performance with GPU acceleration
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Background Blur

Apply blur to the background wallpaper for a modern, focused look.

### Blur Settings

- **Toggle** - Enable/disable background blur
- **Effect** - Applies a gaussian blur to the wallpaper behind your video
- **Use Case** - Creates depth of field, making your video content stand out

<Note>
Background blur only affects the wallpaper, not your video content. Your recording remains sharp and clear.
</Note>

## Motion Blur

Add cinematic motion blur to smooth fast movements.

### How Motion Blur Works

- **Enable** - Toggle motion blur in the effects panel
- **Rendering** - Applied during export to frames with rapid movement
- **Performance** - Uses GPU acceleration for efficient processing

<Warning>
Motion blur increases export time as it requires additional frame processing. Use it selectively for best results.
</Warning>

## Border Radius

Round the corners of your video for a modern aesthetic.

### Border Radius Settings

- **Range** - 0 to 48 pixels
- **0** - Sharp corners (default)
- **8-16** - Subtle rounding
- **24-48** - Pronounced rounded corners

<Accordion title="Recommended border radius values">
- **1080p exports**: 12-24px
- **720p exports**: 8-16px
- **4K exports**: 24-48px

Scale the radius based on your output resolution for consistent appearance.
</Accordion>

## Padding

Control the spacing between your video and the edges/background.

### Padding Settings

- **Range** - 0 to 200 pixels
- **0** - Video fills the entire frame
- **50** - Default, comfortable spacing
- **100-200** - Large padding for background emphasis

<Info>
Padding creates space around your video where the wallpaper or background color is visible. Combine with shadows for a floating video effect.
</Info>

## Combining Effects

Effects work together to create professional-looking videos:

### Recommended Effect Combinations

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Modern & Clean">
    - Gradient wallpaper
    - Shadow intensity: 40
    - Background blur: On
    - Border radius: 16px
    - Padding: 60px
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Minimal">
    - Solid color background
    - Shadow intensity: 20
    - Background blur: Off
    - Border radius: 8px
    - Padding: 40px
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Dramatic">
    - Dark gradient wallpaper
    - Shadow intensity: 80
    - Background blur: On
    - Border radius: 24px
    - Padding: 80px
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="No Effects">
    - No wallpaper (transparent)
    - Shadow intensity: 0
    - Background blur: Off
    - Border radius: 0px
    - Padding: 0px
    
    Results in just your raw video content.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Real-Time Preview

All effects update in real-time in the editor:

- **Instant feedback** - See effects as you adjust settings
- **Scrub through timeline** - Effects visible during timeline scrubbing
- **Playback** - Effects render during video playback

<Note>
Preview quality is optimized for performance. Final export will render effects at full quality.
</Note>

## Performance Considerations

### GPU Acceleration

Open Screen uses WebGL for effect rendering:

- **Hardware accelerated** - Utilizes your GPU for fast rendering
- **Fallback support** - Software rendering available if GPU unavailable
- **Optimized pipelines** - Effects are rendered in efficient batches

### Effect Complexity

Effect | Performance Impact
-------|-------------------
Wallpaper | Low
Shadow | Low-Medium
Background Blur | Medium
Border Radius | Low
Padding | Minimal
Motion Blur | Medium-High

<Warning>
Enabling multiple high-impact effects may slow down export times, especially at 4K resolutions. Monitor export progress and adjust settings if needed.
</Warning>

## Export Quality

Effects are rendered at full quality during export:

- Shadows use high-quality gaussian blur
- Wallpapers are scaled to match output resolution
- Border radius is anti-aliased for smooth edges
- All effects respect the selected export quality setting

## Keyboard Shortcuts

No direct keyboard shortcuts for effects, but you can:

- Use **Tab** to navigate between effect controls
- Use **Arrow keys** to adjust slider values
- Use **Enter** to confirm changes

## Best Practices

<Accordion title="Match effects to content type">
**Tutorials**: Minimal effects, focus on content clarity

**Marketing videos**: Use shadows, blur, and wallpapers for polish

**Demos**: Subtle effects that don't distract from the demonstration
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Consider export resolution">
Scale effect values (padding, border radius) based on your export resolution. What looks good at 720p may appear too small at 4K.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Preview before exporting">
Always preview your full video with effects enabled before starting a long export to ensure the look matches your expectations.
</Accordion>
